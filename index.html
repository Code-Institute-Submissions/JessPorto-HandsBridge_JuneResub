Welcome to Handsbridge!

Handsbridge Project is a charity campaign by the church Assembly of God ireland ministry, in Newbridge, this campaign aims to raise funds for people in Brazil and Ireland, initially it was designed for people affected by the pandemic, but we are happy to extend this campaign for all people in need , who are struggling financially to survive in Brazil and Ireland. Primarilly, we are focused on provide food and essential items for people in need, but with your help we would like to give other types of suport, like mental health care (learn more here).
