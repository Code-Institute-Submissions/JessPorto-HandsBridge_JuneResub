Who we are

We are one of the churches that God has put in this country to preach the Gospel of Jesus. Jesus is the way and the truth and the life. We're a vibrant multicultural community of Christians from all social backgrounds, nationalities and walks of life, who've found freedom, purpose and true love in Christ Our vision is to be a visible demonstration of the Kingdom of God on Earth. Our goal is to build a church of faithful and committed God-loving and God-fearing people of all backgrounds, nationalities and cultures; a centre where all would feel welcomed, loved and cared for and where diversity is celebrated and not demonised.
